Urban Informalities: Street Vendors

This map focuses on the Chicago Neighborhood of Little Village. It attempts to review what spaces have the most compatible features for street vendors. The goal is to identify spaces that have higher compatibilities in hopes that pedestrian improvements can be prioritized in these areas.

You can learn more about efforts to improve opportunities for street vendors in the City of Chicago here.

Stree vending is a crucial component in the informal economy of many parts of the world. The importance of this economy is best captured most recently by the Netflix Documentary "Street Food"
